Events

At Coastal Bean, we love bringing people together. From special meals to live music, there's always something happening at our café!

Meal Specials

  1. Monday Breakfast Special - Free coffee with any pastry.
  2. Wednesday Lunch Deal - Sandwich + juice combo at a discount.
  3. Friday Dinner Treat - Half-price desserts after 6 PM.

Café Functions

We host private functions such as birthday parties, small corporate meetings, and book clubs. Our cozy space can be reserved for groups with catering options available.

Seasonal Specials

  1. Summer: Fresh fruit smoothies and iced coffee specials.
  2. Winter: Hot chocolate and hearty soup of the day deals.
  3. Festive Season: Christmas-themed pastries and spiced lattes.

Live Bands

Every Saturday evening, enjoy live performances from local bands and musicians. Relax with great music, delicious food, and your favorite coffee.

Other Occasions

From poetry nights to art exhibitions, Coastal Bean supports local talent. Follow our events calendar for upcoming occasions and community gatherings.
