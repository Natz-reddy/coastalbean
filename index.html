Welcome to Coastal Bean

Welcome to Coastal Bean, your neighborhood spot for great coffee, fresh pastries, and good vibes. We believe that a café should be more than just a place to grab a quick cup of coffee — it should feel like home. That’s why we’ve created a cozy space where friends can meet, families can relax, and new ideas can come to life.

Every cup we serve is brewed with care using only the finest beans, and every bite of food is prepared with love. Whether you’re here to work, catch up with friends, or simply enjoy a moment to yourself, we’ve got a seat waiting for you. Thank you for being part of our story — we can’t wait to serve you and make your day a little brighter.
